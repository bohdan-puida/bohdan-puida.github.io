Artificial Intelligence (AI) is usually defined as the science of making computers do things that require intelligence when done by humans. Research in AI has focussed chiefly on the following components of intelligence:

  • Learning
  • Reasoning
  • Problem-solving
  • Perception
  • Language-understanding
